## What Does the 3-Day Golden Triangle Tour Include?

Our 3-day Golden Triangle tour is the most popular package we offer, covering the three iconic cities of North India -- Delhi, Agra, and Jaipur -- in a compact, well-paced itinerary. This tour is designed for travelers who want to experience the highlights of India's cultural heartland without spending a week doing it.

Over three days and two nights, you will visit some of the most important monuments in Indian history: the [Taj Mahal](/attractions/taj-mahal), [Red Fort](/attractions/red-fort), [Amber Fort](/attractions/amber-fort), [Humayun's Tomb](/attractions/humayuns-tomb), and more. The total driving distance is approximately 700 km, covered in an air-conditioned private vehicle with an experienced driver.

## What Makes Our Golden Triangle Tour Different?

We are not a booking aggregator -- we are licensed local guides who personally lead every tour. Our guides hold certifications from the Ministry of Tourism, Government of India, and have an average of 10+ years of experience guiding visitors through these three cities.

Every tour is private, meaning you travel only with your group. There are no waiting for other tourists, no rigid schedules, and no commission-driven shopping stops unless you specifically request them.

The tour price starts at $299 per person (based on double occupancy) and includes accommodation, all monument entry fees, and private transport. This represents excellent value when compared to the $500-800 charged by international booking platforms for similar itineraries.

## Who Is This Tour Best For?

The 3-day Golden Triangle is ideal for first-time visitors to India, travelers with limited time, and anyone who wants a comprehensive introduction to Mughal and Rajput heritage. It is also an excellent option for business travelers based in Delhi who have a long weekend to spare.

For those who prefer a more relaxed pace with additional stops and deeper exploration, we recommend our [5-day Golden Triangle tour](/tours/golden-triangle-5-days), which adds extra time in each city and includes visits to Mehtab Bagh, Nahargarh Fort, and a Chandni Chowk food walk.
