## Why Book the Overnight Agra Tour?

Our 2-day Agra overnight tour is the ideal middle ground between a rushed [same-day trip](/tours/same-day-agra-from-delhi) and a longer [Golden Triangle itinerary](/tours/golden-triangle-3-days). By spending one night in Agra, you gain the luxury of experiencing the [Taj Mahal](/attractions/taj-mahal) in multiple lights -- morning, sunset from [Mehtab Bagh](/attractions/mehtab-bagh), and optionally at sunrise the next day.

The overnight format also allows you to visit [Fatehpur Sikri](/attractions/fatehpur-sikri) on day two, a UNESCO World Heritage Site that same-day travelers almost always miss. Starting at $179 per person, the tour includes accommodation near the Taj Mahal, all entry fees, private transport, and a licensed guide.

## What Does Each Day Look Like?

Day one begins with an early morning pickup from your Delhi hotel. After the 3-hour drive to Agra, you visit the Taj Mahal (spending 2-3 unhurried hours), followed by [Agra Fort](/attractions/agra-fort) and Itimad-ud-Daulah (Baby Taj). The afternoon culminates with sunset at Mehtab Bagh, where our guides help you capture the perfect photograph of the Taj Mahal reflected in the Yamuna River.

Day two offers the option of a second sunrise Taj visit -- many of our guests say this dawn experience, with fewer crowds and ethereal light, is even more magical than the first. After a hotel breakfast, you drive to Fatehpur Sikri, Emperor Akbar's abandoned 16th-century capital, before returning to Delhi by mid-afternoon.

## Who Is This Tour Best For?

The overnight Agra tour is perfect for photography enthusiasts who want multiple Taj Mahal sessions, couples seeking a romantic getaway from Delhi, and history buffs who want to include Fatehpur Sikri. It also suits travelers who prefer not to endure a 14-hour day of driving and sightseeing that the same-day trip demands.
