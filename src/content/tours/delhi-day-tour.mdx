## What Does the Delhi Day Tour Cover?

Our full-day Delhi sightseeing tour covers the essential highlights of both Old and New Delhi in a single, well-structured day. You will experience the contrast between the Mughal-era walled city of Shahjahanabad (Old Delhi) and the grand colonial avenues of Edwin Lutyens' New Delhi -- two cities that together tell the story of India's capital across seven centuries.

Starting at $69 per person, the tour includes a licensed English-speaking guide, private air-conditioned vehicle, all monument entry fees, and hotel pickup and drop-off anywhere in Delhi. This is our most affordable tour and the perfect introduction to India's capital.

## How Is the Day Organized?

The morning is devoted to Old Delhi. We begin at the [Red Fort](/attractions/red-fort) (closed Mondays), the massive 17th-century Mughal fortress where India's Prime Minister hoists the flag each Independence Day. From there, we walk to Jama Masjid, one of India's largest mosques, and continue with a cycle rickshaw ride through the narrow lanes of Chandni Chowk -- the 400-year-old bazaar where spice shops, sweet makers, and textile merchants operate much as they did in the Mughal era.

After a lunch break (our guide will recommend restaurants based on your taste and budget), we shift to New Delhi. The afternoon covers [India Gate](/attractions/india-gate) with a drive along Kartavya Path toward Rashtrapati Bhavan, followed by [Humayun's Tomb](/attractions/humayuns-tomb) and [Qutub Minar](/attractions/qutub-minar).

## Who Is This Tour For?

The Delhi day tour is ideal for travelers who are spending one day in the capital before heading to [Agra](/cities/agra) or [Jaipur](/cities/jaipur), business travelers with a free day, and anyone who wants an expertly guided introduction to Delhi. It also serves as day one of our [Golden Triangle itineraries](/tours/golden-triangle-3-days).
