## What Does the Same-Day Agra Tour Include?

Our same-day Agra tour from Delhi is our most booked single-day experience. It is designed for travelers based in Delhi who want to see the [Taj Mahal](/attractions/taj-mahal) and [Agra Fort](/attractions/agra-fort) without an overnight stay. The round trip covers approximately 460 km, completed in a single day with a private air-conditioned vehicle and a licensed guide.

We pick you up from your Delhi hotel early in the morning, drive to Agra via the Yamuna Expressway (approximately 3 hours), spend 5-6 hours sightseeing, and return you to Delhi by evening. The tour price of $89 per person includes all monument entry fees, private transport, and a licensed guide in Agra.

## How Is the Day Structured?

The morning begins with a hotel pickup between 5:00 and 6:00 AM. After the expressway drive, you arrive at the [Taj Mahal](/attractions/taj-mahal) by 8:30-9:00 AM (or earlier for sunrise visits during summer). Our guide walks you through the history and architecture of the monument over 2-3 hours.

After the Taj Mahal, we visit [Agra Fort](/attractions/agra-fort), located just 2.5 km away. The fort tour takes about 1.5 hours. Lunch is at a recommended restaurant near the Taj (not included in the tour price, allowing you to choose your preference and budget).

The afternoon includes a visit to Itimad-ud-Daulah, often called the "Baby Taj" for its exquisite marble inlay work that predates the Taj Mahal. Time permitting, we can add a quick stop at [Mehtab Bagh](/attractions/mehtab-bagh) for river-side views of the Taj. The return drive to Delhi typically gets you back to your hotel by 7:00-8:00 PM.

## Who Should Book This Tour?

The same-day Agra tour is ideal for business travelers in Delhi with a free day, cruise passengers on a port stop, and travelers whose itinerary does not include an Agra overnight. It is also popular with returning visitors who have already explored Delhi and Jaipur and want to revisit the Taj Mahal.

For those who prefer a more leisurely experience, our [overnight Agra tour](/tours/agra-overnight-from-delhi) adds a sunset at Mehtab Bagh and an early-morning Taj Mahal visit the following day.
