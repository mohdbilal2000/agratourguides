## Why Choose the 5-Day Golden Triangle Tour?

Our 5-day Golden Triangle tour is designed for travelers who want more than a highlights reel. While the [3-day tour](/tours/golden-triangle-3-days) covers the must-see monuments, the 5-day version adds cultural depth -- a Chandni Chowk food walk through 17th-century bazaars, a dedicated sunset photography session at Mehtab Bagh, panoramic views from Nahargarh Fort, and unhurried time to explore each city's hidden gems.

The total route covers approximately 700 km across three cities, but with two additional days, you experience it at a comfortable pace. Mornings are dedicated to major monuments, afternoons to lesser-known treasures, and evenings to relaxation or cultural experiences.

## What Does Each Day Look Like?

Day one immerses you in the sensory overload of Old Delhi -- the narrow lanes of Chandni Chowk, the grand Red Fort, and a street food walk that introduces you to parathas, jalebis, and spiced chai. Day two bridges Old and New Delhi before the scenic drive to Agra along the Yamuna Expressway.

Day three is the centerpiece: a sunrise visit to the [Taj Mahal](/attractions/taj-mahal), a morning at [Agra Fort](/attractions/agra-fort), and a sunset at [Mehtab Bagh](/attractions/mehtab-bagh) -- giving you the Taj Mahal in three different lights within a single day. Day four transitions to Rajasthan via [Fatehpur Sikri](/attractions/fatehpur-sikri) and culminates at [Amber Fort](/attractions/amber-fort). Day five explores Jaipur's old city before departure.

## Who Should Book the 5-Day Tour?

This tour is ideal for photographers, culture enthusiasts, food lovers, and anyone visiting India for the first time who wants a comprehensive and memorable introduction. Families with children also benefit from the relaxed schedule, which avoids the early starts and long drives that make the 3-day version more demanding.

Starting at $499 per person (double occupancy), the 5-day Golden Triangle includes 4 nights of accommodation, all entry fees, private transport, and a dedicated licensed guide for the entire journey.
